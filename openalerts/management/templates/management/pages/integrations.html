Embeds and Integrations

Include OpenAlerts on your own site

We recommend using Pym.js to load our embeds on your website. The following reusable code snippets have been customized for your organization, and require pym-loader.js to be present on the page. To install pym-loader.js, you can use the following CDN import:


<script src="https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/npm/pym.js@1.3.2/dist/pym-loader.v1.min.js" integrity="sha256-ymoVGOsQ+8XK3XbddMhMVSKz4y4RWVWd8G+PWJ+MZYk=" crossorigin="anonymous"></script>

Once you have pym-loader.js installed, you can use any of the following embeds.
